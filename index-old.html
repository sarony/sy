Saron

Saron Yitbarek is an entrepreneur and founder of CodeNewbie, the most supportive community of programmers and people learning to code, now owned by DEV. She's also a developer, speaker, and podcaster.

Podcasts

Saron hosts and produces the CodeNewbie Podcast and the Basecs Podcast. She also hosts Command Line Heroes, the Red Hat podcast.

Speaking
